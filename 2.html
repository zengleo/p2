The Dataset

This dataset shows pretest and posttest scores of students from various educational backgrounds.

Source

The fields used in this project are as follows:

school_setting: Whether the school is located in an urban, suburban, or rural area

school_type: Whether the school is public or not

teaching_method: Whether the students are taught using standard or experimental methods

n_student: Number of students in the classroom

gender: Gender of the student

lunch: Whether the student qualifies for subsidized lunch

pretest: Student's pretest score

posttest: Student's posttest score
